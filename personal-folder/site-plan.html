Overview

Site Purpose

The Student Motivational Hub is a simple, uplifting web app designed to help students stay positive, motivated, and focused. It includes three dynamic features: a daily motivational quote generator, a goal tracker that uses localStorage, and an interactive mood slider that gives custom encouragement.

Audience

The target audience includes college students, teenagers, and adults looking for quick motivation, stress relief, and simple personal tracking tools.

Brand Guide

Color Palette

  • Primary: #4CAF50 – uplifting green
  • Secondary: #f5f5f5 – light background
  • Text Color: #333 – dark gray text

Typography

  • Headings: Poppins, sans-serif
  • Body Text: Roboto, sans-serif

Content

Examples of Actual Text & Imagery

These examples represent what users will see on the site:

  • Sample Motivational Quotes: “You’ve got this!” “Small steps still move you forward.”
  • Example Goal: “Finish my math homework.”
  • Completion Message: “Goal completed! Great job!”
  • Mood Slider Messages: Low mood: “You're stronger than you think.” Mid mood: “You're doing great — keep going.” High mood: “Love the energy!”

Dynamic Elements & LocalStorage Explanation

The goal tracker stores the user's daily goal in localStorage. When the user types a goal and clicks “Save Goal,” it is saved and restored when the page reloads. When the user marks the goal as completed, localStorage removes the saved goal.
